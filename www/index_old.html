Traveling by van from Rhode Island to Seattle, we will explore how the lives and relationships of three women (Sandra, Annabelle and Shannon) intertwine providing each other with the support required to accept a transgender family member, to live transgender in Seattle's community and society at large, and to undergo gender confirmation surgery. This documentary looks deeply into the invisible transgender world demystifying their lives, voicing their hopes and fears and more importantly, bringing dignity to their experiences.
