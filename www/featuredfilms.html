VENUS, PRIESTS, AND SUPERWOMEN

“Venus, Priests, and Superwomen is an illuminative and colorful peek into the brave journey of Providence College’s students’ struggle to present the Vagina Monologues. This film admirably embraces verities (truth), Providence College’s motto…Bravo to this new generation of sheroes!” -- Alix Olson, Spoken Word Artist/Activist

IN THE SHADOW OF THE CROW

“The 2003 Smoke Shop Incident is central to the documentary, as it is to the Tribe today. Chief Sachem Matthew Thomas was among those arrested. On camera he compares his people being arrested on land ‘sacred to us’ to a synagogue being invaded by state police.” -- Bill Rodriquez, The Phoenix

NOT YOUR MAMA'S ROLLER DERBY

“Not Your Mama’s Roller Derby probes deep to answer questions that neophytes want to know about roller derby such as what drives women to skate and is this a dangerous sport? Yes, it’s not your mama’s roller derby— there’s no alligator pits or staged fights—roller derby is edgy and totally misunderstood.”-- Alexia Kosmider, Producer Not Your Mama’s Roller Derby

SAPPHO'S FIRE

“‘The film is unique in showing both the difficult paths taken by older lesbians and the love of life they embrace in their elder years,’ said J.M. Sorrell, Director of the North Hampton SAGE affiliate. It’s inspiring for the young and old, and it’s historically important storytelling.” -- Suzanne Wilson, Daily Hampshire Gazette.
