Shifting Vision’s new documentary work-in-progress, TransJourney explores how three women’s relationships are connected and made stronger, because of each other.

Three years ago Annabelle, who now lives in Seattle, was identified as a male at birth, came out to her family and friends as female and began her transition towards life as a woman. Shannon, who also lives nearby in Washington, has become friends with Annabelle as well as Annabelle’s mother, Sandra. Shannon had provided Sandra with guidance on ways to care for her daughter after Annabelle’s gender confirmation surgery. Now Shannon is preparing for her own gender confirmation surgery, but struggles with illness as well as sexual discrimination.

In August 2013, Shifting Vision’s film crew will travel by van from Providence, Rhode Island to Seattle to film Annabelle and Shannon’s world. Sandra, Annabelle’s mother, will travel with us to Seattle to visit her daughter and to meet Shannon for the first time. We also will gain insight into how Sandra copes with this life-altering event and strives to remain an integral part of her daughter's new world -- 3,000 miles away!

TransJourney speaks to all of our desires to receive affirmation and to explore new borders whether it’s with existing family members or with new acquaintances whom we embrace in our life. We hope that TransJourney will enable all individuals to see themselves and others differently, and will inspire many not to be afraid to be themselves, who ever they choose to be.
